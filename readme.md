# Amaze
By @Camleaf, @gheatherington, @Daniel-Li-08, @spedwurd, @solicht88, @TaranFlora3856

**Your neighborhood's delivery shortcut.**

Amaze makes group ordering easy, efficient, and surprisingly fun.  
From reducing shipping costs to helping the environment, Amaze connects neighbors so everyone benefits.

---

## What is Amaze?

> *“The only thing we have to fear is a big package!*

Amaze helps neighbors organize **group orders** so everyone saves money on shipping.  
Prime holders can share their benefits and even earn back their subscription cost by coordinating deliveries in their area.

Whether you're managing a group buy, starting a shared cart, or joining an existing order, Amaze keeps everything organized and effortless.

---

## Why Amaze?

### We Reduce Costs  
Group orders significantly **lower per-person shipping** and help everyone save more.

### We are Greener  
Fewer separate deliveries → **less packaging** and **lower emissions**.  
Good for you, your wallet, and the planet.

### We provide Community Convenience  
Coordinate with neighbors for simple **local pickup** or **doorstep delivery**.  
Amaze brings communities closer while making shopping smoother.

---

## Features

- Create, join, and manage **shared shopping carts**
- View, track, and update cart items in real time
- Organize group orders with neighbors
- Share Amazon Prime benefits or shipping discounts
- Clean, modern, responsive UI

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React / Vite
- **Browser Extension** - HTML/CSS
- **Language:** Javascript
- **Styling:** CSS / Tailwind
- **Backend:** Python-Flask
- **Database:** Firebase
- **Deployment:** Vercel  

Unlike many other new projects, Amaze, in the spirit of helping the environment, uses 0 Generative AI in it's operations
---

## Using Amaze

### Prerequisites
- Node.js v16+  
- npm or yarn  

### Installation

To use Amaze, you need to first download our browser extension from our latest [Release](https://github.com/Daniel-Li-08/Amaze/releases/tag/Release), then load it into chrome!
